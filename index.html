Twigs, our newly refurbished self-catering cottage is nestled in the beautiful south Cotswold village of Langford. A stone's throw from the famous towns of Burford and Lechlade-upon-Thames, this is the perfect location for your Cotswold holiday or mini break.

Within the grounds of the owner's home, this lovely cottage for 2-4 people, also has access to a large garden, with seating area, views of picturesque countryside ans is well located for local walks, pubs and restaurants that are there to be enjoyed.

Recently renovated with luxurious and stylish interiors, the cottage is stocked with everything you need for a relxaing getaway.
